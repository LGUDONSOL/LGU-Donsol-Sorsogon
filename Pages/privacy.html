Privacy Policy

Municipality of Donsol Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how the Municipality of Donsol collects, uses, and protects information provided through this website.

1. Purpose of this Website

This website is intended to provide public information about the Municipality of Donsol, including tourism, municipal services, announcements, and contact information. It does not process online transactions or payments.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information only when voluntarily provided through the inquiry form, such as:

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Contact Number
  • Subject and Message

3. How We Use Your Information

Information submitted through the inquiry form is used solely for:

  • Responding to inquiries
  • Providing requested information
  • Improving communication with the public

4. Data Protection

The Municipality of Donsol takes reasonable steps to protect personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

5. No Online Transactions

This website does not handle payments, applications, or financial transactions. Users are advised not to submit sensitive personal or financial information.

6. External Links

This website may contain links to external platforms such as Facebook and Google Drive. These sites operate independently and have their own privacy policies.

7. Data Privacy Compliance

This website aims to align with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173) of the Philippines, ensuring that personal data is handled responsibly and securely.

8. Changes to this Policy

The Municipality of Donsol reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy as needed. Changes will be reflected on this page.

9. Contact Information

For questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, you may contact:

  • Email: admin@donsol.gov.ph
  • Phone: (056) 411-2544
  • Address: Barangay Awaii, Donsol, Sorsogon
