About Donsol

A municipality known for eco-tourism, public service, and community pride

Donsol is presented here as a public-information website with a tourism identity that reflects its strongest attraction while staying clear, welcoming, and easy to navigate.

Municipality Profile

The Municipality of Donsol, located in Sorsogon Province, Philippines, is a leading eco-tourism destination internationally known for its sustainable whale shark (butanding) interaction program.

Guided by environmental protection and community participation, Donsol promotes responsible tourism that supports conservation and local livelihoods.

Beyond tourism, this website is built to help the public easily access information, understand municipal services, and connect with the local government through a simple, friendly, and informative online experience.

Vision

A premiere agri-tourism destination in Bicol where God-loving, disaster resilient and adaptive communities, thriving in a safe and orderly built and natural environment and a progressive economy under an inclusive governance system.

Mission

We advocate intense implementation of tourism guidelines and develop world-class tourism facilities, provide effective delivery of basic services, promote sustainable development, enhance people’s participation, ensure judicious utilization of resources, and practice committed governance toward the realization of our vision.

Privacy Policy

Simple privacy information for website visitors

This policy is written in plain language so the public can easily understand how inquiry details are handled.

What information may be collected

This website may collect only the information you choose to provide through the inquiry form, such as your name, email, contact number, subject, and message.

How inquiry details are used

Inquiry details are intended only for communication and response purposes. This website does not process online payments or public transactions.

Mayor’s Message

Welcome to Donsol — a place blessed with natural beauty, rich culture, and the warm hospitality of our people.

It is my honor to invite you to discover our town, a destination known not only for its world-renowned butanding experience, but also for its peaceful communities, beautiful coastal treasures, and the genuine kindness that makes every visit memorable. Donsol is more than a tourist destination — it is a home of proud traditions, hardworking people, and meaningful experiences that connect visitors to nature and community.

As your Mayor, I believe tourism plays a vital role in the growth of Donsol. It creates opportunities for our local communities, supports livelihoods, promotes our culture, and inspires us to protect the natural resources that make our town truly special. Our vision is to continue building a tourism industry that is sustainable, inclusive, and beneficial to every Donsolanon.

Through the efforts of our local government, community partners, and tourism stakeholders, we are committed to making Donsol more welcoming, more vibrant, and more prepared for the future. We want every guest who comes here to feel the heart of our town — from the beauty of our environment to the sincerity of our people.

To our visitors, thank you for choosing Donsol. We welcome you with open arms and invite you to experience the best of what our municipality has to offer. To our fellow Donsolanons, let us continue working together to showcase our town with pride and preserve it for generations to come.

May this website serve as your guide in exploring the wonders of Donsol.

Mabuhay and welcome to Donsol!

Hon. Ralph Walter “Chuck” R. Lubiano
Municipal Mayor
